Accessibility

Click "Learn More" on any card to expand

Wheelchair Friendly

All our tour packages include wheelchair-accessible accommodations, transport, and routes.

What We Provide

  • Wheelchair-accessible hotels & resorts on all packages
  • Adapted transport vehicles with ramps and secure seating
  • Pre-screened routes free from major accessibility barriers
  • Dedicated mobility assistant available upon request
  • Priority boarding on all flights and excursions

How to Request

Mention your mobility requirements in the booking form or call our concierge at +92 300 1234567 at least 48 hours before departure.

Visual Assistance

Audio guides and braille materials available upon request for visually impaired travelers.

What We Provide

  • Professional audio guide devices in 10+ languages
  • Braille itineraries, menus, and destination booklets
  • Sighted guide assistance at major attractions
  • Large-print documents and high-contrast materials
  • Screen-reader compatible digital travel documents

How to Request

Contact our concierge at concierge@packngo.store with your specific requirements at least 72 hours before your tour.

Hearing Support

Sign language interpreters and written communication support for all group tours.

What We Provide

  • Certified sign language interpreters (ASL, BSL & others)
  • Written communication cards for every destination
  • Captioned video content for all in-room entertainment
  • Vibrating alarm systems in all partner hotels
  • Visual emergency alert systems throughout the trip

How to Request

Specify your interpreter language preference in the booking form. Requests must be made 5 days in advance to ensure availability.

Family Friendly

Special arrangements for families traveling with young children or elderly members.

What We Provide

  • Baby cots, high chairs and child-safe room setups
  • Kid-friendly meal options at all partner restaurants
  • Stroller-accessible routes at every destination
  • Supervised children's activities on select packages
  • Elderly-friendly slow-paced tour options available
  • Family room upgrades at preferential rates

How to Request

Include the ages of all travelers in your booking. Our team will tailor the itinerary to suit your family's needs perfectly.

Medical Support

Dedicated medical assistance and first-aid trained staff on all expeditions.

What We Provide

  • First-aid certified tour guides on every group trip
  • Emergency medical evacuation coverage included
  • Refrigeration facilities for insulin and temperature-sensitive medications
  • Pre-trip health screening and consultation available
  • Coordination with local hospitals at each destination
  • 24/7 medical helpline throughout your journey

How to Request

Share your medical conditions confidentially via concierge@packngo.store. All information is handled with strict privacy.

24/7 Concierge

Round-the-clock personal concierge for travelers with special needs and requirements.

What We Provide

  • Dedicated personal concierge assigned to your booking
  • Available via phone, WhatsApp, and email 24 hours a day
  • Real-time problem solving for any travel disruptions
  • Special dietary and cultural requirement coordination
  • Last-minute booking changes handled with priority

FAQ's

How do I book?

Use our booking form or contact our concierge directly by phone or email.

What is included in a tour package?

All packages include accommodation, guided tours, airport transfers, and 24/7 concierge support. Flights may vary — check the specific package details.

Can I customise my itinerary?

Absolutely. We specialise in bespoke travel. Contact our concierge and we will tailor every detail to your preferences and schedule.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14+ days before departure receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7–14 days receive a 50% refund. Please contact us for special circumstances.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included by default but is strongly recommended. We can assist you in arranging comprehensive coverage through our trusted partners.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 6 or more travellers receive special pricing. Reach out to our team for a personalised group quote.
